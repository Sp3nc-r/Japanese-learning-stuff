こんにちは! Greetings in Japanese.

As with any language, one should learn the introductions and greetings. Hello in Japanese is こんにちは (kon'nichiwa). One should use this greeting during mid-day.

When speaking on the phone, instead of using the previously stated phrase, the more correct phrase would be もしもし (moshimoshi). This word also means hello, but should not be used outside of phone conversation. Let's go over other greetings.

  • おはよう (ohayō) - Good morning.
  • さようなら (sayōnara) - Goodbye.
  • 今晩は (konbanwa) - Good evening.
  • おやすみなさい (oyasuminasai) - Goodnight.
